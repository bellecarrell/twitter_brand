Identifying Self-Promoting Twitter Accounts

View Instructions

Identify self-promoting Twitter accounts and what types of things they tweet about


We are interested in identifying English-speaking Twitter users who use Twitter to promote their expertise or careers online. For each HIT, follow the link to a Twitter user's profile summary, and perform two tasks. (1) Classify this account as either belonging to a "self-promoting" user: someone who uses Twitter primarily to promote their expertise, online presence, or career, or one of the three other accounts: inactive account, a non-English speaker, or personal account (account is used to talk about personal life, more than promoting their career).
