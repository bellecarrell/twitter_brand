Identifying Self-Promoting Twitter Accounts

View Detailed Instructions and Examples

Identify self-promoting Twitter accounts and what types of things they tweet about


We are interested in identifying English-speaking Twitter users who use Twitter to promote their expertise or careers online. For each HIT, follow the link to a Twitter user's profile summary, and perform two tasks. (1) Classify this account as either belonging to a "self-promoting" user: someone who uses Twitter primarily to promote their expertise, online presence, or career, or one of the three other account types: inactive account, a non-English speaker, or personal account (account is used to talk about personal life, more than promoting their career). (2) For self-promoting users, identify which categories the user specializes in. You should use all parts of their profile summary page to make the judgment: their profile image, user name, description, and recent tweets. Use the checkboxes to the left to select all relevant categories, and the radio buttons to the right to select the most relevant category.
